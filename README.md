Political Penguin facilitates the comparison of randomly generated districts by providing a rapid and interactive analysis platform, enabling users to visualize and evaluate various redistricting plans efficiently.

Users can select between Ohio, Virginia and Florida to take a look at over 100,000+ randomly generated distrct plans.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/629fe4df-9481-4537-a482-c8272513db13)

District plans are compared to one another using several distance measures.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/20b9d6db-a5e3-4458-b4d0-6b258f8922d0)

Other helpful information is also available to the user in determining whether a district is potentially gerrymandered or not. 
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/74f52f90-89b7-4687-b349-32419f6ab169)

Users can also compare district plans to one another to further aid in analysis.
